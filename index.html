About Me

Hi, I'm Eric, and I'm currently in the Computer Science and Business Administration Double Degree program at the University of Waterloo and Wilfrid Laurier University.

I am a passionate programer with experience in full stack web development and Android app development, using a variety of languages, frameworks, and APIs. I enjoy learning about algorithms, participating in hackathons, and programming with new technologies.

In my free time, I enjoy photography, graphic design, building computers, and playing board games.
